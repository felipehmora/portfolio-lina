Lina Marea

Venezuelan pianist

I am Lina Esther Marea Peñalver, a Venezuelan pianist born in 2000. I study at the Liceu Conservatory in Barcelona and have performed in significant venues in Caracas and Barcelona. As a finalist in the 2014 XII National Piano Competition Silvia Eisenstein and a guest soloist with the Municipal Orchestra of Caracas, I am also dedicated to teaching piano and music theory.

About me

I am a venezuelan pianist and teacher living in Barcelona, Spain. I am currently studying my Master's degree in Piano Performance at LUCA School of Arts in Leuven, Belgium. I have more than eighteen years playing and performing solo piano music and chamber music in various formats in Venezuela and Spain.
